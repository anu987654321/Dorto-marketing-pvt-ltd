WHO WE ARE?/ About Us

In this hectic world, we do not have much time to indulge in a few chores that affect our lifestyle and we tend to ignore them due to our busy schedule. Your car runs through a lot of dust and pollution every day, which makes its hygiene miserable. Here comes "Dorto" with its unique service that takes care of your car and provides you a full package of car wash and Sanitization.

We use the latest equipment and technology to take care of your motor car combining it with our experienced and highly qualified specialists; we guarantee you the best service that you expect for your car. Therefore, take a chill pill because your vehicle is in safe expert hands with “Dorto”. We offer doorstep services at a nominal price that means you can call us anywhere in Ranchi and the best team will be at your doors. All you need to do is just provide us an electric point and few buckets of water and you get your car brand new again. We offer services like Car wash, Interior detailing, Exterior detailing, AC sanitization, Engine bay cleaning and Windshield treatment. DORTO has been designed to be user-friendly and customer-centric for ease of its users to book our services. Prioritizing professional quality services at affordable prices to the flexibility of the customers makes DORTO a reliable service provider. At “Dorto”, we are committed to provide hassle free maintenance services to our customers based on our extensive research. We promote water conservation with our techniques and focus on saving your time and money too. The ECO (waterless) wash offered by DORTO takes it a step further towards environment-friendly car wash solutions.
